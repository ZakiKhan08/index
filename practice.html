Iron Man, also known as Tony Stark, is a fictional superhero appearing in Marvel Comics and its associated media. He's known for his high-tech suit of armor and his role as a key member of the Avengers. The character's origin story often involves his capture and subsequent creation of the Iron Man suit to escape, leading him to become a superhero. Tony Stark is a genius, billionaire, playboy, and philanthropist, who uses his wealth and intellect to develop advanced technology, including his iconic armor. Batman is a superhero who appears in American comic books published by DC Comics. Batman was created by artist Bob Kane and writer Bill Finger.
